Master Alan

Power: "Demigod of the elements"

Bio

Master Alan Enfield is more commonly known by his ability to cast and manipulate Fire, Ice, Wind and Lighting, granting him the title of demigod. He was first discovered by Alfonse Frost in his childhood days at the orphanage, where they forged a strong friendship despite their differences. Alfonse Frost helped him to overcome his fears and encouraged him to accept himself for who he truly is and embrace his power.

When Alfonse Frost was eventually chosen by a rich family to be taken-in, he promised Alan that he would come back for him, promise that he kept, saving every cent drom his fortune to go back and get his dear friend out of there.

They grew together along with Ellie, and together forged the idea to create a school where other gifted could learn to accept and properly use their abilities, without the discrimination of the outside world.

Alan is currently the teacher oc C-3 Classroom and lives within the school in his own private quatters but it is known later in the game that he has his own manor out in the north, a secret place that functions as a base for the player in the late part of the story when certain conditions are met.
