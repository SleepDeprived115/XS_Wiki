Akemi Orbeck

Power: Rejection type C

Bio

Akemi is a very serious, cold and dismissive student who spends most of her free time at the school's library.

She's one of the most if not the actual most acomplished students at this school, with many records set in various disiplines and awards piled up somewhere, definitely not her own room as it's of the plainest amongst the plainest of rooms. But the truth is that, instead of keeping the awards for herself she sends them to her parents, who are less than impressed by her acomplishments no matter how much effort she puts into it. But just like with any other challenge, this won't stop her from keep trying.

It is currently unclear since when or why her parents treat her in such a strict and cold way.
