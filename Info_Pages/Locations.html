Locations: Inside the school

Overview

There are various locations in the school that the player can visit, within and outside the main building.
Some of these are not normally accesible and need to be unlocked first by performing certain actions or by completing parts of the min story.


Great garden

The great school garden is a very large area in the north'east side of the school's main entrance, it's main purpose is of course, to offer the students a large, safe and healthy recreational green area.

To enter the garden, face the school's main entrance and follow the concrete path that leads left, you'll see the school's oval (and the soccer field) on your right as you walk down this path all the way up to a big gate, go through it to walk into the garden's main path.


  • Fountain's crossroad - In the "middle" of the garden. This is actually the first area that one can see upon starting to walk the garden's path is this fountain.




  • Garden's dinning area - At the northern side of the garden. Facing the Fountain as you first walk into the garden's road, take the path on left side and follow it straight up until you see another stone arc gate.




  • Garden's gazebo - At the southern side of the garden. Facing the fountain, take the path on the right side to reach this area.




  • Garden's heart - At the northeast side of the garden, this area can be easily accessed through the oval-shapped path on the east side of the garden, just from there, easily take the path on the left and that should lead you to this side of the garden. This is where you can easily find Headmaster Ellie every morning and late night.




  • Garden's lakeview - At the northwest side of the garden. Following the same path as you can do with the dinning area, except that you'll see how the path branches off to the left, go down this path to reach the lake.






Outdoor swimming pools

These swimming pools are the most commonly used for recreation as they are more spacious and have sunbath chairs nearby.






Baseball Stadium

This stadium is first introduced as where the sports club used to practice this same sport until the team disbanded. Following the proper steps, the player can rebuild this baseball team and participate in matches.





Volleyball field

This is where the sports club normally practices this same sport, twice a week (Tuesdays and Thursdays).





Soccer field and school oval

This is where the sports club normally practices this sport, once a week (Mondays only).





Basketball field

This is where the sports club normally practices this sport, once a week (Wednesdays only).





Archery target practice area

This place is not very often visited by anyone until Chapter 2, when Yumiko Kanamori starts visiting the place each night.





Artificial lake

This is more of a decorative lake made to give the school an aditional natural touch, this place is visited by various students in different days, most commonly by Megan Travers, who claims to have a friendship with a small turtle that she feeds.





Shrine

Shrine that serves as a place to mourn the dead or/and pray, most commonly visited by Amy Hollister, Emi Kutsuki and Allie Cooper.





Showers

This small section is located right next to the outdoor swimming pools, making them pretty convenient for quick showers before entering the pool.





First floor of the school's main building.



Conference room A

The biggest conference room in the school, pretty useful as well for the school plays and other studdent gatherings as it can host the total amount of studdents at the same time.





Principal's office

This is where the player can find Headmaster Ellie from 9am - 4pm.





Teacher's lobby

This is where the player can locate any teacher outside class times, except weekends, as the room is locked.





Infirmary

This is where the player can fully restore their party's HP for free, with Lilianna Uphill's power at any hour of the day and night even on weekends, except at times between 2am - 5am (In which case, the player has to talk to her instead at her room "GR-1").





Indoor school pools

These pools are most commonly used for training purposes, or an alternative to swimming outdoors.

Second floor of the school's main building.



Gym A

This is the school's main gymnasium, although it is most commonly used for obstacle climbing training. The course is mentioned to have been designed by Sarai Ravyn, sports club president.





Gym B

This gymnasium is mostly used for exercise and yoga routines, the player can find some sport's club members pretty early in the morning here, even on weekends.





Gym C

Rather reffered to as the boxing room, is equipped almost uniquely to host boxing matches. Members of sport's club can be found here on Fidays.





Locker room and showers

This large dual room section hosts both a locker room and showers, conveniently closely to both Gym A and Gym C.

Third floor of the school's main building.





C-A



Also known as "Classroom A", this is where studdents from class A take their daily lessons, Class A students normally are those who are still relatively new and require more beginer-level guidance to control their powers, classes taken here are mostly about accepting and learning to live in harmony with their power. In order to move on from this class, the gifted student must show genuine acceptance and control their power's basic impulses/reflexes.



C-B



Also known as "Classroom B", this is where students from class B take their daily lessons, Class B students have mostly been those who have spent over six months within the school, moving on from this class requires a major improvement of control over their abilities, classes taken here are mostly about physical training.



C-C



Also known as "Classroom C", this is where students from class C take their lessons from Monday through Thursday, students from this class are mostly taught and orientated to their proper re-integration to society. Volunteers from this class are tasked with the highest-difficulty missions as well, and after another 6 months, they decide when to graduate and move out from the school.
