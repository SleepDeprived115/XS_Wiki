Locations outside the school

Overview

There are various locations in the game that the player can visit outside the school, generally for bonding.
Some of these are not normally accesible and need to be unlocked first by performing certain actions or by completing parts of the min story.


Arcade

At the arcade, one has access to several minigames, and it's a nice place to bond with other students during your free time. this is also the place where Zullie Hisai works at during evenings.
