Charms

Overview

Charms are powerful equppable amulets that grant the player various effects or boosts.



The number of charms that can be equipped at the same time is directly dependant on the total of avaliable charm slots within the apparel loadout currently equipped.



Each charm is rare as they are unique and can may only be obtained in a single way, once per playthrough, with improved versions of these charms only obtainable on new game plus.


The aquisition of these charms is generally through the completion of of special events, missions or Bonding.



Some charms provide special effects when equipped at the same time, while others may actually produce adversary effects.
