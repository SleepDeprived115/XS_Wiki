Game mechanics: Battle

Overview

When players face enemies in imminent combat during their missions, they engage in "Battle mode", where the player can perform different actions in order to deffend themselves or/and go on the offensive.

Depending on the type of mission, the player is allowed to fight alongside up to 2 fellow XS students that they may have set as companions.
During battle mode, the player and their allies may be inflicted with different status effects that may come as a result of being exposed to a speciffic type of attack, these effects can be increased/lowered or completely negated by some pieces of Apparel or Charms.


The basics of Battle

In battle mode, the player can make use of their character's power and weapon to deffend themselves, and so does any avaliable ally that they may have set as companions.

Each power has their own "skill tree" that depends drastically on the power's nature and relies on the character's stats for their unlocking and further boosting. For a general overview of the existing powers visit the powers page, or for a more detailed info on only the playable powers, seek the power skill trees page.

Each weapon has it's own damage output types, (Slash, Blunt, Thrust), and see the weapons page for more details.

If you're having trouble at defeating some enemies, pay attention to their weaknesses to better understand how to beat them, and same goes for when you feel like your squad is taking too much damage, changing apparel equipment to something more resistant to the damage type you're facing could mean the difference between victory and defeat.



Damage types



  • Physical damage types: Slash, Blunt, Thrust.
  • Elemental damage types: Fire, Lighting, Frost
  • Cognitive damage types: Mental
  • Mystical damage types: Spiritual

Status effects

When the character is exposed long enough to certain conditions or certain attacks (especially if we're talking about elemental attacks), they are likely to be aflicted by a status effect.

Status effects are normally caused by enemies. Down below it is displayed all different status effects that can be buffed/inflicted upon a character, playable or not.
