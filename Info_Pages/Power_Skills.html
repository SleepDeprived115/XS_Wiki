Power skill trees

Overview

Skills are the power abilities of the gifted, as the character levels-up, more skills can be unlocked and their effects can be enhanced by continuing to invest level-up points on the stats that power those skills.

Below you'll find a table that offers details on each Power's skill tree, the stats required to unlock it and how much (in %) their effectiveness can be boosted.

*Some power skill trees may vary in diversity, depending on the power type*
