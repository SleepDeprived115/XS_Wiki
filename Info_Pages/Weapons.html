Weapons

Overview

The denominated "Weapons" are tools of battle, used by the gifted of XS that have volunteered to participate in tasks/missions for the school. Their use has been authorized for the protection of one another against the potential dangers that they can face in their missions, which can range from simple tasks up to rescue and protection of other living beings from inminent threats.

The players themselves do not always start with a speciffic tool/weapon upon creating their character however, a free tool/weapon can be selected by completing the first week of school.

More tools/weapons can be obtained through game progress such as by completing special missions or by buying them with in-game currency.
